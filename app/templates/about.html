About us

Your life as a scientific researcher should be a adventure into the understanding of nature. But if you are like most, intense pressure to produce more results for the publications. Squeezing the life out of you.

This squeeze, and overwhelming stress you feel in the lab. Biohack is here to help you. We are helping researchers like you. We can not make you free from your stress and pushing for results, but we can make your life little easier.

We do this because science conducted in joy is better for you, it’s better for science and it’s better for the world you serve as a scientist.
